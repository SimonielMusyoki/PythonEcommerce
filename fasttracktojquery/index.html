Your order can only be cancelled when it is yet to be confirmed or if the order has not been accepted by the vendor.

If the order has already been accepted by the vendor or picked up by the delivery agent you are no longer able to cancel. Kindly note that you will be charged the full value of the order if you still want to go ahead with the cancelation

If your order was canceled before it was accepted by vendor or asigned to a DA your refund will be credited back to the original payment source used for the purchase.

Please note that the refund processing time may vary depending on the payment method:

  • If you paid via mobile money, the refund will be issued to your mobile money account within 24 hours.
  • If you paid via bank card, it may take 3-5 business days for the refund to appear in your account.
  • If you paid with a voucher, your refund voucher will automatically become valid, and you can use it again before it expires.
  • If you paid via jumia store credit, the refund will be credited to your Jumia account as store credit within 24 hours
